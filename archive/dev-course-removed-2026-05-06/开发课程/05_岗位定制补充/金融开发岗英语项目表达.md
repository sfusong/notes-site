# 金融开发岗英语项目表达

这份材料的目标不是把你训练成英文演讲选手，而是让你在金融开发岗面试里，至少能稳定完成这 4 件事：

1. `用英文做简短自我介绍`
2. `用英文介绍当前工作和岗位方向`
3. `用英文讲 1 到 2 条核心项目`
4. `在被追问时不立刻卡住`

这份材料强调的是：

`可开口、可复述、可面试使用`

不是追求特别书面或特别地道。

---

## 一、英语准备的正确目标

对你来说，英语这块最重要的不是：

- 用特别复杂的句子
- 背很多高级表达

而是：

- 句子短
- 结构稳
- 关键词对
- 不会一开口就散

所以你后面所有英文表达，都尽量遵循这个原则：

`simple, structured, repeatable`

---

## 二、英文自我介绍最稳的结构

建议统一按这 4 句来：

1. 你现在是谁
2. 你主要做什么
3. 你最相关的经验是什么
4. 你为什么对这个岗位感兴趣

### 可直接复述版

“My name is Shifusong. I am currently working in fintech at a wealth management company. My work is mainly related to financial systems, including product-related systems, TA-related workflows, interface integration, testing, and production support. I am particularly interested in developer roles in financial institutions because I already have hands-on experience in asset management system projects, and I want to continue growing on the engineering side.”

---

## 三、如何用英文介绍你现在的工作

### 可直接复述版

“Currently, I work on fintech-related projects in a wealth management company. My work is mainly about internal business systems, especially product systems and TA-related processes. I have been involved in system building, interface coordination, UAT support, production rollout, and some automation-related work.”

---

## 四、如何用英文讲“产品生命周期管理系统”

### 1 分钟简版

“One of my core projects is a product lifecycle management system. It was built for special wealth management products, such as dividend closed-end products and target-yield products. The main purpose was to turn lifecycle-related business rules into system logic, so the system could calculate important events and send reminders to relevant roles. On this project, I participated in both backend and frontend work. The backend side was mainly about business rules and event logic, while the frontend side was mainly about page interaction and API integration.”

---

## 五、如何用英文讲“TA 自动化测试”

### 1 分钟简版

“Another project I often talk about is TA automation testing. The core idea was to simulate the interaction between distributors and the TA system, especially the process of request files coming in and confirmation files going back. The purpose was to make sure that after each TA version upgrade, the core business flow was still stable. My role was more on scenario design, business rule clarification, and promoting the implementation, rather than being the sole low-level developer.”

---

## 六、如何用英文讲“TA 批前预警稽核平台”

### 1 分钟简版

“Another relevant project is a pre-batch warning and audit platform for the TA system. Its purpose was to move key checks forward before the nightly batch process. It included two types of checks: transaction-related pre-batch warnings and static parameter checks. The value of the platform was to expose issues earlier, instead of letting them become bigger problems during batch processing or clearing. My role was mainly on requirement discussion, rule clarification, and driving the implementation forward.”

---

## 七、英语里怎么讲你的角色，才不会讲过头

你最稳的英文角色表达有这些：

- `I participated in...`
- `I was involved in...`
- `I worked on...`
- `My role was mainly...`
- `I contributed to...`

不建议频繁使用：

- `I led the whole project`
- `I independently built the entire system`
- `I was the sole developer`

---

## 八、如果英文面试官问：你的技术栈是什么

### 可直接复述版

“On the engineering side, I have mainly worked with Java, Spring Boot, Python, React, SQL, and Linux-based environments. My experience is strongest in business system development, interface coordination, batch-related workflows, and production support in financial institutions.”

---

## 九、如果英文面试官问：为什么想投这个岗位

### 可直接复述版

“I want to apply for this role because it matches both my domain background and my technical direction. I already have experience in asset management-related systems and financial workflows, and I want to continue growing as a developer in this domain, especially in roles that combine engineering delivery with real business systems.”

---

## 十、如果被追问听不太懂，怎么稳住

先学会这几句过渡句：

- `Let me think for a second.`
- `If I understand your question correctly...`
- `From my experience, I would say...`
- `My role was mainly on...`
- `The core point was...`

---

## 十一、面试可直接复述的一版英文总自我介绍

“My name is Shifusong. I am currently working in fintech at a wealth management company. My work is mainly related to internal financial systems, including product-related systems, TA-related workflows, interface integration, automation-related work, and production support. One of my core projects is a product lifecycle management system, where I worked on both backend and frontend parts using Spring Boot and React. I was also involved in TA-related projects such as automation testing and pre-batch warning checks. I am interested in this role because it matches both my financial domain background and my technical direction, and I want to continue growing as a developer in financial institutions.”
